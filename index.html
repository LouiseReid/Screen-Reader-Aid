VoiceOver Companion

A live co-pilot for testing web accessibility with VoiceOver.

As you move focus in your browser, this window shows what VoiceOver is likely to announce, flags potential issues, and suggests what to try next.

It works with web content in a browser (not native apps), and the announcement is a close approximation — exact wording varies by VoiceOver version and system language.

Step 1 of 3

First, grant macOS Accessibility permission so the app can read the focused element.

  1. Click Open Accessibility Settings.
  2. Turn on VoiceOver Companion in the list.
  3. Come back here and click Re-check.

Step 2 of 3

You’re all set. Here’s the layout:

  • Inspector — what VoiceOver announces for the focused element, potential issues, and what to try next. Updates live as you move focus.
  • VoiceOver keys — a cheat sheet of common commands for testing.
  • Settings — live tracking, always-on-top, and opacity.
