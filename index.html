VoiceOver Companion

To show what VoiceOver announces, this app needs macOS Accessibility permission.

  1. Click Open Accessibility Settings below.
  2. Turn on VoiceOver Companion in the list (in dev mode it appears as Electron).
  3. Come back here and click Re-check.

If it doesn’t switch right after granting, quit and relaunch the app.
